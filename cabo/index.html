CABO

A card game of memory and deception

Quick Rules

  • Goal: End with the lowest total card value
  • You start with 4 face-down cards; peek at your bottom 2
  • Each turn: draw from deck or discard pile
  • Then: swap, match, discard, or use a power card
  • 7-8: Peek at one of your cards
  • 9-10: Peek at an opponent's card
  • J-Q: Swap any two cards on the table
  • Black Kings (♠ ♣): Spy & Swap — pick one of your cards and one opponent's card, peek at just one, then swap or keep. Worth 13 points in hand (worst card!)
  • Red Kings (♥ ♦) are worth 0 points!
  • Jokers (★) are worth −1 point — the best cards in the game! No power, just hold them.
  • Call Cabo when you think you have the lowest score!
